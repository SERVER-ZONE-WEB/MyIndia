Political System

  • Parliamentary Democracy with Federal Structure
  • World's Largest Democratic Nation
  • Constitution adopted on January 26, 1950
  • Universal Adult Suffrage (One Person, One Vote)

India's Constitution is the world's longest written constitution with 395 articles and 12 schedules.

Government Structure

  • President - Head of State
  • Prime Minister - Head of Government
  • Bicameral Parliament (Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha)
  • Independent Judiciary System

Electoral System

  • Independent Election Commission
  • Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs)
  • Regular State and National Elections
  • Multi-Party System
