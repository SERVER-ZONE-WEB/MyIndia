Ancient India (3300 BCE - 1200 CE)

  • Indus Valley Civilization (3300-1300 BCE) - Advanced urban planning and architecture
  • Vedic Period (1500-500 BCE) - Development of Sanskrit and early Hindu texts
  • Mauryan Empire (322-185 BCE) - Emperor Ashoka's reign and spread of Buddhism
  • Gupta Empire (320-550 CE) - Golden Age of Indian science, art, and literature

Did you know? The world's first university, Takshashila (now Taxila), was established in ancient India around 700 BCE.

Medieval India (1200-1800 CE)

  • Delhi Sultanate (1206-1526) - Islamic influence and architectural developments
  • Vijayanagara Empire (1336-1646) - Preservation of Hindu culture in South India
  • Mughal Empire (1526-1857) - Art, architecture, and cultural synthesis
  • Maratha Empire (1674-1818) - Rise of regional powers and resistance

Modern India (1800-Present)

  • British Colonial Period (1858-1947) - Social reforms and national awakening
  • Independence Movement (1857-1947) - Non-violent struggle and national unity
  • Post-Independence Era (1947-Present) - Democracy, development, and modernization

India gained independence on August 15, 1947, marking the end of British colonial rule and the beginning of the world's largest democracy.
